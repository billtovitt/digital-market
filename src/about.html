Monalisa - A Binance Smart Chain-based NFT Marketplace for Digital Artwork (tested with BSC Testnet, contract address is 0x3D16BFEf1FF573567E1b43F6579cb055e8df34FF)

Executive Overview

A platform to create and exchange NFTs on the Binance Smart Chain. This project is part of the Binance Hackathon to create a decentralized application on the Binance Smart Chain.

MVP

Our approach for this project is first to develop an MVP with the following objectives:

  • Buy and Sell NFT on the platform
  • Drag-and-drop a file and create the corresponding NFT

    • Our Vision of The Market and The Future of Our Project

    Wallet Supplying and Ease of Use

    Although much effort has been made to democratize the NFTs, there are still many points to be improved, especially concerning the ease of use for creating tokens for digital artwork. We will bridge the gap to to allow users to be onboarded from their traditional payment methods and make it no-friction, easy to use platform.

    Tutorial

    Tutorials to use the platform will either be directly integrated as a sandbox and explanatory videos to explain in a simple way the use of our platform.

    Redeemability and Provenance

    Beyond natively digital assets, NFTs are digital representations of physical non-fungible assets. This specific use case also allows independent artists or certain luxury brands to create a certificate of origin attesting to the characteristics in a digital way.

    Marketing

    Even if there are more and more NFTs users, there are still a lot of acquisition levers possible on platforms like TikTok or Instagram.
